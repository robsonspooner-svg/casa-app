## 13. FAQ Promises (Binding)

### 13.1 What Casa Is
- AI-powered property management software (not a licensed PM)
- Handles: tenant communications, rent collection, maintenance coordination, compliance
- Through automation, not human staff
- Owner remains property manager of record
- Casa is the "intelligent assistant"

### 13.2 Escalation & Safety
- Built-in escalation paths for every scenario
- Urgent maintenance: coordinates emergency tradespeople immediately + notifies owner
- Complex decisions (legal, large expenses): always seeks owner approval
- Owner sets thresholds for auto-handle vs sign-off

### 13.3 Legal Compliance
- Designed to comply with tenancy legislation in NSW, VIC, and QLD
- More states coming
- All notices, lease documents, processes follow state-specific requirements
- Generates legally compliant documents
- Ensures correct notice periods and procedures

### 13.4 PM Transition
- Helps transition from current PM smoothly
- Owner notifies current PM, Casa handles the rest
- Imports tenant details
- Sets up rent collection
- Sends welcome message to tenants
- Most transitions < 1 week

### 13.5 Inspections Options
- Starter: Casa schedules, owner conducts using guided app
- Pro: Professional inspections included
- Hands-Off: Professional inspectors coordinated on behalf
- All tiers: Casa processes condition report and flags issues

### 13.6 AI Capabilities
- Reads tenant messages
- Categorises maintenance requests
- Drafts compliant notices
- Makes smart decisions based on preferences
- Learns from corrections
- Gets better over time

### 13.7 Cancellation & Data
- No lock-in contracts
- Cancel anytime from account settings
- Exports all data: tenant records, payment history, documents
- Can transition to another solution without losing anything
